## 📄 One-Pager: Sovereign AI Governance
**Tagline:** Open Source Engine + Enterprise Dashboard for Verifiable AI Decisions

### The Challenge
Regulated organizations (Financial Services, Healthcare, Government) face a "Privacy Paradox": 
They must prove their AI systems are compliant with the **RBI Free-AI mandate**, **EU AI Act**, and **SEC Examination Priorities**, but they cannot risk sending sensitive trade secrets, PII, or internal prompts to a third-party auditor or cloud provider.

### The Anchor Solution
Anchor resolves this by decoupling the **Regulatory Hub** from the **Enterprise Data Plane**.

- **Free Core Engine**: Local runtime auditing + cryptographic proofs (Open Source).
- **Sovereign Hub**: Decentralized "Metadata-Only" ledger for jurisdictional oversight.
- **Privacy First**: The Hub stores the *proof* of compliance; the *data* stays on your private servers.

### Key Architecture: Hub & Spoke
- **Metadata-Only Hub**: Stores cryptographic fingerprints and governance outcomes. 100% PII-free.
- **Private Spoke**: Stores raw inference data behind your firewall.
- **Forensic Relay**: Secure, on-demand WebSocket pulls for authorized auditors only.

**Verifiable. Sovereign. Scalable.**
